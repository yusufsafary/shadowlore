The World of ShadowLore

About the Anthology

Ancient lore. A forgotten kingdom. A king who cannot remember his own name.

The Game

ShadowLore is a browser-based text adventure — a game of words, choices, and consequences. You read, you choose, and the story responds. There are no graphics to render, no controllers to configure, no downloads to install. The entire experience runs in your browser, on any device, for free.

The first chapter, The Hollow King, places you at the sealed gate of Valdris — a city frozen in time, its king hollowed by a bargain he did not fully understand. What you do next is entirely your decision. The story has multiple paths and four different endings, each reflecting the choices you made along the way.

The World: Velundra

The world of Velundra was shaped by beings called the Thirteen Architects — ancient entities who wove reality from shadow and starlight. When the last Architect fell, they did not die. They became the land itself: its mountains, its rivers, its deep silences. And in those silences, their memory survived.

The kingdom of Valdris sits at the edge of a territory once called the Shadow March — lands where the Architects' influence was strongest, where bargains between mortals and old powers were once commonplace. King Morvain was not the first ruler to attempt such a deal. He was simply the most recent to discover that the old powers define their terms differently than mortals do.

The Characters

  • King Morvain (Edran) — A ruler who bargained for immortality and received continuity without participation. He has been watching his kingdom freeze for seven years, unable to act, unable to stop, unable to end.
  • Mira — Former royal cartographer. She made maps of a kingdom she loved and stayed behind when everyone else fled. She has been waiting in a tavern for three years for someone capable of reaching the king.
  • The Archivist of Dusk — A shadowy figure whose annotations appear at the margins of in-game documents. The Archivist witnessed everything. They will not tell you what to do. They will tell you what happened.

The Anthology Format

ShadowLore is designed as an ongoing anthology — a collection of interconnected stories set across different eras of Velundra. The Hollow King is Chapter I. Future chapters will explore different corners of the world, different characters, different bargains. Characters from one chapter may appear as legends — or warnings — in another.

The anthology grows as new chapters are written and released. Updates will be posted here.

About the Project

ShadowLore is an independent creative project — a text adventure built with care for the form. It believes that the best stories are not about heroes who always win, but about people trying to do something meaningful in a world that is complicated, broken, and still worth caring about. If a story made you feel something you didn't expect: that was the point.

"The Architects did not create Velundra to be beautiful. They created it to be honest. And honesty, as any shadow will tell you, is rarely comfortable."
— The Archivist of Dusk, Preface to the First Anthology
