Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2025

What We Store

ShadowLore uses browser localStorage (not traditional cookies) for two purposes:

NamePurposeDuration
sl_game_saveStores your game progress — current node, flags, stats, history — so you can resume where you left off.Until cleared or new game started
sl_cookie_consentRemembers whether you accepted or declined the consent banner.Until cleared by user

This data stays entirely on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

What We Do NOT Use

  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Analytics tracking (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Cross-site tracking of any kind
