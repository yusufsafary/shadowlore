What is ShadowLore?

A Text Adventure Played in Your Browser

No downloads. No accounts. No redirects. ShadowLore is a complete dark fantasy text adventure — fully playable here, right now, on any device.

📖

Rich Branching Story

Every choice you make changes what happens next. Explore Valdris your way — through the tavern, the market, or straight to the castle gates.

🎭

Multiple Endings

Four distinct endings await: from dark bargains to true redemption. The path you take and the secrets you uncover unlock different fates.

💾

Auto-Save Progress

Your journey is saved automatically. Close the browser, come back later — the Hollow King will still be waiting.

📱

Mobile-First Design

Built for mobile from the ground up. Play on your phone, tablet, or desktop — the shadows follow you everywhere.

🌑

Dark Fantasy World

Valdris is a city sealed for seven years, its king hollowed by a bargain with something ancient. Every corner has a story. Every choice has a cost.

⚡

Free to Play

No payment. No account. No in-app purchases. ShadowLore is a complete experience, free to anyone who dares enter the gate.

The Story So Far

The Kingdom of Valdris

The Setting

A City Sealed for Seven Years

Valdris was sealed when its king made a bargain for immortality and received something worse. Its streets are empty. Its people are gone. Its gate stands ajar.

The King

King Morvain, the Hollow

He is present the way an echo is present: a record of something that once made noise. He has forgotten he was human. He cannot sleep. He cannot dream.

Your Role

The Witness Who Arrived

You are not a hero. You are someone who came here with reasons they have not yet spoken aloud. What you do with the king — and his kingdom — is entirely up to you.

Ready?

The Gate Is Open. The King Is Waiting.

No experience required. No wrong choices — only different consequences. Your story begins now.
